About Me

Hi there. My name is Cody Clegg. I recieved a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology in 2016, after which I worked in factory automation and electrical systems testing for a major vehicle manufacturer for a few years and discovered a passion for software development. I feel confident that this will help me in my ability to develop robust and meaningful applications and websites.
